pig game

this is a game where there are 2 dice and 2 players and the goal is to get to 100 (default value which can be changed by entering a number on to the "final score" box)
during each turn, the numbers rolled by the player is added to their "current" box
a player can keep rolling the dice until they roll a 1 on either dice
once they roll a 1, they lose all of their "current" points and it's the next player's turn
they can save their "current" points by clicking "hold"
once they chose to "hold", their "current" points are added to their total points and it's the next player's turn
